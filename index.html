About This Resource

We are dedicated to providing up-to-date information on the leading assistive technologies available today.

Whether you are looking for screen readers, magnification software, or tactile displays, our goal is to help you find the right tools to navigate the digital landscape with confidence and independence.

Assistive Technology Resources

Explore industry-leading software and hardware solutions.

Software Solutions

JAWS®

Job Access With Speech (JAWS) is the world's most popular screen reader, developed for computer users whose vision loss prevents them from seeing screen content or navigating with a mouse.

ZoomText®

ZoomText Magnifier/Reader is a fully integrated magnification and reading program tailored for low-vision users.

Fusion®

Fusion provides the best of both worlds – ZoomText, with its screen magnification and visual enhancements, coupled with the power and speed of JAWS.



Hardware Solutions

Monarch Tactile Device

The Monarch is a revolutionary dynamic tactile device that brings multi-line braille and tactile graphics to the fingertips of blind and low-vision users.

BrailleSense 6

The BrailleSense 6 is the most powerful braille notetaker on the market, designed to help you communicate, connect, and create.
